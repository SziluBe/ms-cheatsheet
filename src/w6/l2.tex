\wt{156} $(X,d)$ seq. compact, $X = \bigcup_{i \in I} G_i$ be any open cover of $X$.

$\forall x,y \in X$ with $d(x,y) < \delta$, $\exists i \in I$ s.t. $x,y \in G_i$.
$\delta$ is called a Lebesgue number for the cover.
